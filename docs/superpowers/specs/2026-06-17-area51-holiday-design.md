# Area 51 Cup School Holiday Refresh Design

## Goal

Refresh the existing bilingual event site for the upcoming school holiday edition while keeping the same event identity, venue, and overall single-page structure.

## Visual Direction

Use a light, sports-poster inspired interface instead of the current dark neon treatment. The page should feel active, crisp, and tournament-ready, with bright court greens, warm yellow/orange accents, and spacious white surfaces. Typography should feel bold and athletic rather than futuristic.

## Content Direction

Keep the event as `2026 Area 51 Cup | Queensland Junior Tennis Championships` and retain Chinese/English language switching.

Update the draft event schedule to:

- Yellow Ball Singles: June 27, 2026
- Yellow Ball Doubles: June 28, 2026
- Green Ball: July 4, 2026 at 9:00 AM
- Orange Ball: July 5, 2026 at 9:00 AM

Keep the player section on the page, but convert it into a clear placeholder stating that final player lists and groups are still being confirmed.

## Page Structure

The new page should remain a single static landing page with these sections:

- Hero with event identity, school holiday framing, venue, and primary CTAs
- Event schedule as clear cards or rows
- Event divisions overview
- Player list placeholder / coming soon block
- Tournament guide / quick notes
- Venue / contact / footer

## Behaviour

Keep the bilingual toggle and dynamic weekday rendering for schedule dates. Remove unnecessary interactions tied to hard-coded player groups. The page should be simple to maintain while participant lists are unsettled.

## Deployment

Prepare the site for Vercel as a static deployment with no build step required.
