📖 参赛及观赛须知 📖 Player & Parent Guide

🎽🍌🥤
赛前准备 Preparation

穿有口袋的运动服（方便携带备用球）。

请备好水、运动饮料、香蕉及零食，防止低血糖。

Wear clothes with pockets (to carry extra balls).

Bring water, sports drinks, bananas & snacks to prevent low blood sugar.
☀️
防晒提示 Sun Safety

即使温度适宜，也请务必涂抹防晒霜，保护皮肤。

Apply sunscreen even if the temperature feels comfortable. Protect your skin!
🤝🤫
比赛礼仪 Etiquette

每场比赛结束后，请在网前与对手握手。🤝

观赛家长：打球期间请保持安静，得分结束后再欢呼加油。🤫

Shake hands at the net after every match. 🤝

Spectators: Keep quiet during points — cheer only after the point is over. 🤫
🟠🟢👨‍👩‍👧
家长协助 Parental Help

橙球组和绿翼组的家长请站在边线旁协助捡球，保持比赛流畅进行。

Parents of Orange and Green groups, please stand by the sidelines to help pick up balls and keep the game flowing.
🏥
健康与安全 Health & Safety

若孩子感到不适或受伤，请立即告知组委会（Eric 教练 / 团队）。

If a child feels unwell or is injured, inform the committee (Coach Eric / Team) immediately.

📈 升级制度 📈 Promotion System

🏆📈🎾
优秀选手将在下届赛事中晋级更高级别！ Outstanding Players Will Be Promoted!

表现出色的选手将在下一届锦标赛中晋升至更高级别。精英组的顶尖选手甚至有机会受邀参加成人组赛事！📈

Outstanding players will be promoted to higher levels at the next tournament! Top performers in the Elite Group will even be invited to participate in Adult Tournaments! 📈

📋 比赛规则 📋 Tournament Rules

🎾 黄球组 Yellow Ball 🎾 Yellow Ball 黄球组
  • 赛制：一盘定胜负（先到6局获胜）。
  • 无Deuce：金球赛制（40-40时直接决胜分）。
  • 决胜局：若局数达到5-5，第11局进行抢5决胜，最终比分为6-5。
  • Format: Full Set — first to 6 games wins the match.
  • No Deuce: Golden Goal rule applies at 40-40.
  • Deciding Game: If score reaches 5-5, the 11th game is a Tie-break (First to 5); final score 6-5.
🎾 绿翼组 Green Ball 🎾 Green Ball 绿翼组
  • 赛制：三局两胜制，前两局为抢8（先得8分）获胜。
  • 决胜局：若局数1-1，第三局进行抢5决胜。
  • 无Deuce：金球赛制（一分决胜）。
  • Format: Best of 3 Sets — each set is a Tie-break (First to 8 points).
  • Deciding Set: If sets are 1-1, the 3rd set is a Tie-break (First to 5 points).
  • No Deuce: No-Ad Scoring (Golden Point).
🎾 橙星组 Orange Stars 🎾 Orange Stars 橙星组
  • 赛制：三局两胜制，前两局为抢8（先得8分）获胜。
  • 决胜局：若局数1-1，第三局进行抢5决胜。
  • 无Deuce：金球赛制（一分决胜）。
  • Format: Best of 3 Sets — each set is a Tie-break (First to 8 points).
  • Deciding Set: If sets are 1-1, the 3rd set is a Tie-break (First to 5 points).
  • No Deuce: No-Ad Scoring (Golden Point).
📊 排名逻辑 · Ranking Criteria 📊 Ranking Criteria · 排名逻辑
  • 1 胜场 — 胜负场次优先 Wins — Most match wins take priority
  • 2 净胜局 — 赢得局数与失去局数之差 Games Won/Lost Diff — Net games won minus lost
  • 3 胜负关系 — 同分选手间的直接对阵结果 Head-to-Head — Direct match result between tied players
  • ★ 黄球最佳第三名 — 先比较与第1种子的对战结果，若仍相同则比较与第2种子的对战结果 Yellow Ball 3rd Place — Compare results against the 1st seed; if still tied, compare against the 2nd seed
